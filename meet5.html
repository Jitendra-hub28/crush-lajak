The Last Time You Looked At Me

If you remember when I spoke to you on the call on 22 APR 2025 around 08:15/08:20, I mentioned a few dates — 10/12/2024, 25/03/2025 & 04/04/2025. If you remember them, that's great.
On those dates, you looked at me like I mattered. Especially on 25/03/2025 — you were in maroon and black formals.

On 10/12/2024, I came to the canteen for lunch but saw you instead — and got excited. Just like in movies, where the hero spots the heroine in a crowded place — that’s how it felt.
You were wearing the same outfit as on the 25th, but somehow you looked even more beautiful. After lunch, my gang was glued to their phones. I didn’t know what to do. As the canteen emptied, I looked at you — and one of your friends noticed and told you. You looked back at me.
That moment… was really scared, disturbed me & so special to me.

On 25/03/2025, between 01:55 and 02:10, you gave me a look I couldn't take my eyes off. It’s hard to forget that.
Later that day, around 07:00 PM near the lift, we crossed paths again. I wanted to talk to you… but I couldn’t. But still, you looked back at me while crossing the road.

Then I don't remember exact date but, it's on friday — a little different. I was waiting outside, hoping to talk to you. I came out early, but you came 10 minutes later. I walked slowly past you.
And then I thought: *If I miss this chance again, I’ll regret it.* So, I turned around and followed you till the station.
I said “hey!” — but you took out your earbuds, and you were on a video call. I just turned back and left.
I think you were talking to your mum — it was around 07:00 PM.

Lastly, on 04/04/2025, after the year-end, you wore a yellow Indian-style outfit — not formals.
Before you left the office, you gave me a look… and that was the last time I saw you.
